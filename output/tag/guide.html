Accessible Mapping Tools for All

Abstract

“A Guide to Meaningful Mapping” is a workbook filled with creative activities for community users to identify their unique perspective of local geography. The guide showcases a variety of innovative map examples to inspire creators. There is also a strong focus on promoting free and open-source mapping tools.
